# Persona: Player (Frontier Actor)

## Who

Single-player who wants a plausible frontier survival sim: map, turns, cause and effect,
households, resources, relationships, and slow improvement. Not looking for empire scale or twitch
gameplay.

## Goals

- Read the land and understand survival pressures
- Select land and assign work without fighting the UI
- Build a household or holding over time
- See persons, agents, resources, and aggregates update predictably
- Trust that the same seed gives the same results

## Frustrations

- Hidden randomness on things they control directly
- Too many menus vs the map
- Unclear what one hex or one patch represents
- Resource changes without an explanation
- Generic historical flavor that does not affect choices

## Success

> "I can see why the household is short on water, select the hexes I want worked, spend the day,
> and understand how the claim is getting safer or more fragile."
