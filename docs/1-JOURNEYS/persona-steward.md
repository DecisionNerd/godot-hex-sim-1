# Persona: Player (steward actor)

## Who

Single-player who wants a small county sim: map, turns, cause and effect. Not looking for empire
scale or twitch gameplay.

## Goals

- Move an **actor** on the hex map (10 m cells)
- End turns and see **persons** and **aggregates** update
- Trust that the same seed gives the same results

## Frustrations

- Hidden randomness on things they control
- Too many menus vs the map
- Unclear what one hex or one patch represents

## Success

> "One hex is about ten meters. I moved my steward, ended the turn, and the peasants rolled
> their actions — same save, same outcome when I reloaded."
